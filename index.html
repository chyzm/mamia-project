Our Story

Mamia Restaurant is Lagos' premier dining destination, where culinary artistry meets timeless elegance. Our vibrant establishment has redefined fine dining in Nigeria by masterfully blending global gastronomic techniques with the rich flavors of West African cuisine.

At Mamia, we curate more than just meals - we craft unforgettable dining experiences. Our award-winning chefs combine locally-sourced, seasonal ingredients with innovative cooking methods to create visually stunning dishes that delight all the senses. From succulent seafood caught daily in the Atlantic to premium cuts of Nigerian-raised beef, every ingredient tells a story of quality and authenticity.

The restaurant's sophisticated ambiance features contemporary African design elements, with warm earth tones, handcrafted decor, and soft lighting that creates an intimate atmosphere. Whether you're celebrating in our private dining room, enjoying cocktails at the marble-top bar, or dining al fresco under our tropical garden canopy, every space offers a unique sensory journey.

Open for lunch, dinner, and Sunday brunch, Mamia has become the preferred venue for discerning food enthusiasts, romantic evenings, business gatherings, and special celebrations. Our mixology classes and chef's table experiences offer guests unique opportunities to engage with our culinary team.

  • Fresh, locally-sourced ingredients
  • Expertly crafted cocktails
  • Intimate and sophisticated ambiance
